Roasted Potatoes

Roasted potatoes complete practically any main they're served alongside.

Ingredients

  • Baby Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley, Rosemary, Thyme
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
